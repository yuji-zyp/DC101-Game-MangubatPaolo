# DC101-Game-MangubatPaolo

title; WHACK-A-MOLE Pixeled-Style

Description;  Whack-a-Mole is a fast-paced arcade game presented in a classic inspired pixel art style. The game features simple, blocky graphics, limited color tones, and retro sound effects that capture the nostalgic feel of old handheld consoles. The screen layout is clean and minimal, making the gameplay easy to understand and visually appealing.

In the game, moles pop up randomly from different holes on the screen, and the player must quickly tap or click them before they disappear. Each successful hit increases the score, while missed moles cost you a heart and test the player’s reaction speed and focus. As the game has different modes like easy,medium,hard and "HELLMODE' increasing the challenge and excitement.

Designed to be simple yet engaging, this Whack-a-Mole emphasizes quick reflexes, timing, and hand-eye coordination. Its retro aesthetic and responsive controls make it enjoyable for both casual players and fans of classic pixel-based games.
